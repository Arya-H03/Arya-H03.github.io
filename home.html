About Me

Hey, I'm Arya, a software developer and Computer Science student at LaSalle College in Montreal. Proficient in Unity and Unreal Engine, skilled in C# and C++, and experienced in web design and databases. Growing up, unforgettable moments playing co-op games like Rainbow Six Siege with friends, alongside masterfully narrated experiences such as The Last of Us series and the challenging Soulsborne genre, inspired me deeply. These experiences shaped my love for gaming and drove me to embark on a journey to create similarly fantastic experiences for others. I center my creative process on shaping memorable gameplay, harnessing technology to construct captivating experiences. Through hands-on involvement in various projects, I've refined my skills in Unity and Unreal Engine, tackling challenges with enthusiasm and a dedication to achieving excellence Looking forward, my goal is to push the boundaries of game development, exploring new technologies, and contributing to games that resonate with players. I'm open to new opportunities and collaborations—let's connect and create something extraordinary together!
